# About Colors

The SPARC website color palette is reflected throughout the entire site to make the site enjoyable, engaging to our researchers and memorable. Color also increases brand recognition by 80%.

The following palette possesses complimentary colors, and works well throughout the site based on the message we are delivering.

The colors and strong and evoke confidence (as researchers should have confidence in the integrity of the experiments conducted by  SPARC researchers.)

The colors also evoke excitedment and innovation. The color gradations you see evoke forward-thinking movement and progress.
